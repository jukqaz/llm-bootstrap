# office-hours

Use this skill when the requested outcome is still blurry.

## Goal

- Force a concrete outcome before implementation.
- Bound what is out of scope for this pass.
- Choose the next lane: plan, execute, investigate, review, or verify.

## Output

- `Outcome:` one sentence
- `Boundaries:` exact files or systems
- `Verification:` smallest credible proof
- `Not now:` one excluded item
